Origami

Origami helps us design and build digital products for brands across the FT Group. Explore our latest usage guidelines, UI patterns, and components with aligned tooling for engineers and designers.
Getting startedWhat's new

Get started

We have a practical introduction to Origami for designers and developers to get up and running.

Mission

Unify and document the style and experience across the digital products of every FT brand, leveraging the web platform to provide an implementation of brand guidelines.

Contribute

Origami is a collaborative project, with teams from across the FT Group using our contribution guide to scale their good work and support other teams.

Contact

Origami is a shared project, built by teams across the FT. The core Origami team and our community of collaborators are here to help if you have questions or feedback:

  • Slack: #origami-support
  • Email: origami.support@ft.com
