Components Introduction

A component is a self contained piece of user interface, from buttons to tables. These may be brought together like building blocks to create new patterns and products. Allowing the team to focus on where their product adds unique value.

In the past, Origami considered “o3-button” a component. In reality this is a “package” of multiple components and pattens, e.g:

  • Primary button
  • Secondary button
  • Ghost button
  • Grouped buttons
  • Pagination
